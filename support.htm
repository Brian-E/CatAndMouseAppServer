Support/Contact info for Cat and Mouse  

 

 

For support-related topics or questions: support@catandmouseapp.com

For general questions/hate mail: questions@catandmouseapp.com

For inquiries or other topics: admin@catandmouseapp.com

If you experience a crash/force close and would like to report it, please include a system log, which can be gathered using an app like Log Collector. There is very little (ok, nothing) that can be accomplished to correct the problem without it.

If it appears that you are no longer receiving messages in the activity window, or not getting mouse points, try quitting all games and re-joining.

For any issues that you cannot workaround, a un-install/re-install will almost always do the trick. Yeah, I know it’s not ideal…

If you purchase and create a game and didn’t get your options correct, or want to change them, use the support email address and include your game name and what values you’d like to have changed (except of course for the game length, unless you pay me more).

Confidentiality

No personally identifiable information is collected by Cat and Mouse. When you are logged into a game, your device mac address, location and Feint userid and name are collected and stored on our server as a means of identifying you as a player. Your location and Feint user information (Name, picture, and score) are made available to other game players via the player map and the game activity window. Once you have quit/logged out of a game this information is dropped, and no permanent records are kept by Cat and Mouse.
